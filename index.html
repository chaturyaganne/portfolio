AdaptAlloc — Adaptive Object Detection

A REINFORCE policy network that dynamically routes video frames to YOLOv8 (fast/low-energy) or RT-DETR (accurate/high-energy) based on a 9-dimensional scene complexity state vector including Shannon entropy and detection uncertainty. Reward = ΔF1 − λ·cost, updated every 8 frames. Live Streamlit dashboard benchmarks the adaptive policy against Always-YOLO and Always-RT-DETR baselines in real time.

Optimization · Healthcare

IMRT Radiation Treatment Planner

Formulated Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT) beamlet optimization as a convex program using CVXPY on the PortPy Lung_Patient_3 clinical dataset. Enforces clinical dose constraints (PTV ≥ 60 Gy, esophagus ≤ 45 Gy, spinal cord ≤ 30 Gy, lung mean ≤ 20 Gy). Introduced a combined spectral regularizer as a memory-efficient surrogate for nuclear norm minimization, reducing effective rank of the fluence matrix while maintaining tumour coverage (D95 ≥ 60 Gy).

CVXPY Convex Optimization PortPy Spectral Regularization
